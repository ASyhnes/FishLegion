Fish's Legion

Le projet "Fish's Legion est un projet de jeu de carte à collectionner au format NFT.
vous pouvez les trouvez sur le site Opensea
Chaque NFT est constitué d'une image au format Png, et d'un fichier 3D au format gtlf.
Vous pouvez voir la version 3D directement sur le site opensea, mais également dans un format plus "propre" sur la page perso du créateur: David Chardon
il existe plusieurs série de 8 cartes, toutes avec un niveau de rareté différentes.
ATTENTION! les cartes rares sont souvent plus forte mais... La forces sans stratégie ne sert à rien!!

les cartes sont constituées de plusieurs information: la force (strengh), l'intelligence (intelligence) et la dextérité (dextérity)

il existe d'autres information:

  • Les série
  • Le numéro de la carte
  • La famille
  • La force

le lore

It's said everywhere that it has been swimming between frequencies since the dawn of time, first soaking up cults, then beliefs, religions. It is sometimes called "Legion", cause it's numerous. We can believe that it is a cloud of fish but it is quite different… Difficult to know if it is real or a fiction, as this concept loses its meaning when you travel between frequencies. One day, man created the internet. Then, the man said to himself that there was better. Internet had become archaic. A new version was released. This version floated, vibrated between different worlds, at the crossroads of frequencies. It's precisely at this junction that the very first image of the cloud was, by the greatest chance, captured. This image looked suspiciously like a cloud of fish…thousands of fish. In fact… It was very difficult to be able to define if this semblance of an image was that of a fish, or something else.
